Chicken Salad

This basic Chicken salad is the best and a family favorite

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup blanched slivered almonds
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon grounded black pepper
  • 2 cup chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.
  2. Mix todether mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in a medium bowl. Toss with chicken, toasted almonds and celery
