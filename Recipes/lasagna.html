Lasagna

Lasagna is a baked dish containing layers of usually cheese, a seasoned sauce of tomatoes and meat or vegetables

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

Here's a very brief overview of whta you can expect when you make homemade lasagna

  1. Make the meat sause
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixure
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instuction
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
